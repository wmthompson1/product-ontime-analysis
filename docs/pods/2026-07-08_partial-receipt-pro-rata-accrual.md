# Pod: Is 1 of 10 items received = 1/10 of the PO accrued?

**Date:** 2026-07-08
**Context:** Partial-receipt accrual (PRA) work — TWM accrual guides, governed view `payables_partialreceiptaccrual_20260708_000004`.

**Q: If 1 of 10 items is received, then 1/10 of PO?**

**A:** Yes, if 1 of 10 items is received, then 1/10 of the Purchase Order (PO) line quantity is considered received and creates an accrual for that portion. The accrual is proportional to the quantity received, so receiving 1 item out of 10 means the accrual reflects the cost of that 1 item, or 1/10 of the total PO line amount. This partial receipt generates a Purchase Receipt Accrual (PRA) for that fraction until invoiced.
